# V3.1.5 POS Actions Menu Patch

This version moves the Prescription button into the POS three-dot Actions popup.

Expected behavior:
1. Open POS.
2. Click the three-dot Actions button.
3. Prescription appears as an action tile inside the popup.
4. Click Prescription to fill out patient/prescription details and upload a file/photo.

This avoids floating buttons and gives a cleaner native POS workflow.
